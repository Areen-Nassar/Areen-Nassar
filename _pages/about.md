Welcome to my academic website.

I am a Postdoctoral researcher in social work at the University of Jyväskylä, affiliated with the Kokkola University Consortium Chydenius. My research focuses on forced migration, well-being, and social sustainability, with particular attention to the experiences of migrants in the Finnish context.

My work explores how individuals in situations of forced migration navigate social structures and pursue well-being. Drawing on the capability approach and interdisciplinary perspectives, I examine the social, institutional, and structural factors that shape opportunities for integration, participation, and a good life.

My research interests include:

- Forced migration and refugee studies  
- Well-being and the capability approach  
- Social work and social policy  
- Migration, integration, and social sustainability  

This website provides information about my research, publications, teaching activities, and ongoing projects.
